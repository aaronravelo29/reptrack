Cataracts dim color saturation and reduce contrast sensitivity, so colors that look "fine" to younger eyes can fade to invisible.
Presbyopia doesn't change color but reduces focus stamina — pure-white pages cause faster eye fatigue, which is why the warm #FAF7F2 background helps.
Every body-text combination above clears WCAG AAA (the recommended level for users with vision impairment).
